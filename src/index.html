About Us

About company

Welcome to Nuray IVF Clinic, where dreams come to life and hope is rekindled.

Today, infertility is not a verdict. The personal experience of the founder of the Nuray IVF Clinic is proof of that: she herself gave birth to wonderful children through in vitro fertilization, and in doing so, found a purpose – to help women who have lost hope with the same diagnosis. That's why our center is named 'Nuray,' which translates to 'Ray of Hope.'

"Since 2014, our center has been bringing happiness to over 2000 families. The right choice of clinic, treating physician, and accurate diagnosis enhance your chances of success

We employ only experienced specialists, and our center is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, facilitating comprehensive assessments including ultrasound diagnostics, colposcopy, hysteroscopy, and genetic testing

We exclusively utilize the most advanced reproductive technologies, tried and tested over time: IVF, IVF+ICSI, PGD, TESE/PESA, surrogate motherhood, egg donation, and artificial insemination

We'll tailor the optimal and effective treatment for you and continue to support you throughout your pregnancy. By entrusting us, you increase your chances of becoming parents

Our mission:
The birth of a healthy baby is the goal that every couple aspiring to create a complete family strives for. Hearing the first cry of your child, holding them close to your chest, witnessing their smile – isn't that true happiness that most women dream of? In our center, your dream will become a reality!

Our goals:
is to turn your dream of a healthy child into a reality!
